Sell digital downloads. Stay close to the people who buy them.

Sell ebooks, templates, audio, or any digital file. Or give them away to grow your list. All in one place with your community, email, and website.

Here's what comes with Digital Downloads on Podia

Everything you need to sell digital files, deliver them fast, and stay close to the people who buy them.

Almost any file type

Upload PDFs, videos, audio, images, ZIPs, text files, design files, and more.

Up to 5GB per file

Each individual file can be up to 5GB in size.

Unlimited files per product

Bundle as many files as you want into a single download product.

Free email delivery

Visitors enter just an email and get the file emailed to them with no account needed (ideal for lead magnets).

Free delivery with signup

Offer the product free in exchange for a customer account..

Paid delivery

Hide the file behind a paywall at any price you set.

Waitlist mode

Collect signups for a product before it's available, without granting access to files.

Customizable landing pages

Each download gets a dedicated landing page you can fully edit.

Customer dashboard

See who has purchased each download, when, and how often they've accessed it.

Bulk customer import (CSV)

Upload a list of emails to grant access to a download in bulk.

Multi-seat / company licenses

Sell packs of seats to organizations that distribute them to team members.

Buyer accounts

Customers can log into a member's area to re-download their files anytime.

Bundles

Add downloads to a bundle with any mix of courses, events, coaching, or community access for one combined purchase.

Include products/items

Grant buyers instant access to bonus products alongside any digital download they purchase.

The internet is getting less human. The way you sell downloads doesn’t have to be.

Your downloads on Podia live with everything else you make: your community, your courses, your email, your blog. Everything and everyone in the same place. Whether they feel like they bought from a creator, or just downloaded something from a vending machine. That's what makes downloads worth coming back for. And the stuff that turns a one-time buyer into a real long-term connection.
